About Ecuadane

Ecuadane specializes in creating custom-designed luxury blankets that tell your organization's story and create lasting impressions.

Our journey began with a passion for craftsmanship and a belief that the finest materials, combined with thoughtful design, can create meaningful connections between organizations and their stakeholders.

Today, we work with prestigious clubs, luxury hospitality brands, universities, and nonprofits to create custom blankets that serve as both practical amenities and cherished keepsakes.
