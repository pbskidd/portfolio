St. Andrews Golf Club

Custom Anniversary Blankets for the Home of Golf



Project Overview

St. Andrews Golf Club, celebrated as the "Home of Golf," commissioned us to create custom anniversary blankets that would honor their rich heritage and tradition while providing members with a keepsake that embodies the club's prestige and history.

The Challenge

Our challenge was to design blankets that would capture the essence of St. Andrews—its historical significance, timeless elegance, and the reverence it holds in the golfing world—while creating a practical item that members would proudly display and use.

Our Approach

We collaborated with the club's leadership to develop a design that honored their legacy while maintaining a sophisticated aesthetic. The resulting blankets featured:

  • The iconic St. Andrews crest and logo
  • A subtle pattern inspired by the historic course layout
  • Anniversary dates commemorating the club's milestone
  • Premium materials that reflect the club's standards of excellence
  • Traditional colors that align with the club's heritage

The Result

The custom anniversary blankets were distributed to members during a special commemorative event. They were received with enthusiasm and have become cherished mementos that connect members to the club's storied past and present.

Many members display the blankets in their homes, offices, and even in their golf carts during rounds at the club, creating a visible symbol of their membership and the club's prestigious heritage.
