University of Notre Dame

Custom Blankets for Academic Excellence

Project Overview

The University of Notre Dame, a leading Catholic research university known for excellence in academics and athletics, partnered with us to create custom blankets for their Mendoza College of Business. These blankets would serve as prestigious gifts for alumni, donors, and special guests.

The Challenge

Our challenge was to design blankets that would reflect the prestige and tradition of Notre Dame while specifically highlighting the Mendoza College of Business. The blankets needed to embody academic excellence and the university's rich heritage in a sophisticated, gift-worthy product.

Our Approach

We collaborated with Notre Dame's brand team and Mendoza College representatives to develop a design that honored their legacy while maintaining a sophisticated aesthetic. The resulting blankets featured:

  • The iconic Notre Dame and Mendoza College branding
  • The university's traditional blue and gold color palette
  • Subtle patterns inspired by campus architecture and academic motifs
  • Premium materials befitting a prestigious academic institution
  • Elegant finishing details suitable for display in homes and offices

The Result

The custom blankets have become coveted gifts for Mendoza College alumni, donors, and distinguished guests. They serve as tangible reminders of recipients' connection to Notre Dame and the Mendoza College of Business, displayed proudly in homes and offices around the world.

The university has reported that the blankets have strengthened alumni engagement and enhanced their donor recognition program, creating lasting connections with the Notre Dame community.

"These blankets perfectly capture the spirit and prestige of Notre Dame and our Mendoza College of Business. They've become one of our most appreciated gifts for alumni and donors, creating a lasting connection to the university."

University of Notre Dame

Alumni Relations Director
