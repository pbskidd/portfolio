Project Overview

The Ferrari Club of the Netherlands wanted an exclusive memento for its flagship event. We collaborated to design a custom blanket featuring the club's logo of the iconic Ferrari stallion atop the bold colors of the Holland flag. The blankets were presented on the opening evening, becoming an instant conversation piece and treasured gear on the tour.
