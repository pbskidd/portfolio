Wounded Warrior Project

Custom Blankets Supporting Veterans and Families



Project Overview

The Wounded Warrior Project, a leading nonprofit supporting wounded veterans and their families, partnered with us to create custom blankets that would serve as both meaningful gifts and symbols of appreciation for their donor community.

The Challenge

Our challenge was to design blankets that would honor the service and sacrifice of veterans while providing comfort and a tangible reminder of the support network available to them through the Wounded Warrior Project.

Our Approach

We collaborated closely with the Wounded Warrior Project team to develop a design that would resonate with veterans and their families. The result was a thoughtfully crafted blanket featuring:

  • The organization's distinctive logo and branding
  • Patriotic elements that honor military service
  • Durable, high-quality materials for everyday use
  • Comfortable dimensions suitable for various settings

The Result

The custom blankets were distributed at various Wounded Warrior Project events and given as gifts to veterans and supporters. They quickly became cherished items that provided both physical comfort and emotional connection to the organization's mission.

The blankets have helped strengthen the sense of community among program participants and have become a visible symbol of the organization's commitment to those they serve.
